KNOW ABOUT US!

Welcome!

To the Community Food Sharing Platform, where we believe in the power of community to drive positive change. Our mission is to connect individuals with excess food to those in need, fostering a culture of sharing, sustainability, and support within our neighborhoods. We believe that no one should go hungry while good food goes to waste. Our platform bridges the gap between those with surplus food and those in need, creating a sustainable and supportive network of sharing and caring.



Our Mission

At the Community Food Sharing Platform, our mission is simple yet impactful: to reduce food waste and combat hunger by providing a centralized space where people can easily donate and access food. We strive to create a system where surplus food is redirected to those who need it most, ensuring that no one goes hungry and valuable resources are not wasted.


"Help Us Build a Better Tomorrow"

What We Do ?

Food Donation: Our platform allows individuals, restaurants, and businesses to easily donate excess food. Donors can find nearby food fridges and contribute surplus food items, ensuring that nothing goes to waste.

Access to Food: For those in need, our platform provides a map of accessible food fridges stocked with donated items. This helps individuals and families struggling with food insecurity to find nutritious food nearby.

Community Engagement: We work closely with local organizations, volunteers, and community members to create a supportive network. Our events and initiatives promote awareness and involvement in food sharing activities.

Towards Achieving

Sustainable Development Goals

Our Impact

Reducing Food Waste

By diverting surplus food from landfills to food fridges, we significantly reduce food waste and its environmental impact.

Supporting Communities

Our platform strengthens community bonds by encouraging sharing and mutual support. It empowers individuals to contribute to a cause that directly benefits their neighbors.

Promoting Sustainability

We align with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals by addressing Zero Hunger (SDG-2), Responsible Consumption and Production (SDG-12), and fostering Partnerships for the Goals (SDG-17).

Join Us

We invite you to be part of this transformative movement. Whether you are a donor, a volunteer, or someone in need, your participation makes a difference. Together, we can build a community where food is not wasted, and everyone has access to the nourishment they deserve.

  • Donate Food: Have surplus food? Find a nearby food fridge and donate.
  • Volunteer: Lend a helping hand by managing food fridges, organizing donations, or spreading the word.
  • Spread the Word: Share our mission with your friends, family, and social networks to help us grow and reach more people.
